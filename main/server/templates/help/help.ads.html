Advertising on Biostar

Advertising on Biostar is open to active users wishing to promote ideas, products and accomplishments that relate to the focus of the site: Bioinformatics and Genomics.

What may be advertised?

Individual users may advertise personal accomplishments, research results, research publications, tools and techniques that they have developed, blogs or websites that they have created, activities that they undertake regardless of whether these have any commercial nature.
The sole restriction of the user initiated advertising is that the person creating the ad must be legally entitled to claim full or partial ownership (at a non trivial level) of the the product/idea/concept that is being advertised.
For advertising outside these conditions please contact the site manager at admin@biostars.org.

Who may run ads?

Depending on the accumulated reputation users have the following options to run their ads:
  • Users with the reputation of 1000 or above (accumulated after 100 votes) may run one ad.
  • Moderators and users with the reputation of 5000 or above may also activate an additional ad for another user.

Creating an Ad

To create a new ad select the New Post button and select the Ad category. The title of your post will constitute the line that will be shown as the ad. The body of your posts will be a regular Biostar post that users can access after clicking the link and should contain further information on the advertised product.
Ads have three stages Stopped, Pending, Active. Upon creating your ad it will have the Pending status and it will need to be activated to be shown within the Ad rotation. Ads in the running state will be shown in the top bar. Every twenty seconds the site loads up the the 10 least shown ads and will select from them for the next 20 seconds. The `rate` field for ads shows the number of times an ads was shown in the last hour. Ideally this number is equal to to all ads. New ads will quickly catch up upon creation.

Launching and Stopping Ads

To see a list of all ads visit the ads tab. If you have sufficient permissions then clicking the word change for your ad will toggle its state to the next one in the succession of Stopped -> Pending -> Active -> Stopped ... Note: depending on your permission rights you may be able to stop your own ad but not reactivate it.

Expiration

Ads expire after one month and at that point will be placed in the Stopped state. Users will need to reactivate the ad by clicking the change.
