Account Merging

We are migrating over accounts from the old BioStar server. The process should be automatic and you should retain all posts and votes. Your reputation is now computed differently, it is now the sum of all upvotes and accepted answers that your posts have received.

Did we make a mistake?

The automated merge may fail to recognize previous users. To request an account merge, please find the user ids for both accounts (top of the profile page) and enter them into the fields below then press the submit button. The Master ID is the account you wish to keep. The Remove ID is the user id for the account you wish to merge into the master. An adminstrator will review the request and if approved will make the change. For speedy approval make sure that your emails on both accounts match.
If this does not solve your problem please email us at admin@biostars.org
