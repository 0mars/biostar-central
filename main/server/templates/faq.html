Voting
Only upvotes exist on biostar, each user has access to up to 10 votes every six hours.
Post Ranking
On most pages posts are ordered by their rank. This rank is a combination of the creation date (more recent dates have higher rank) and the number of votes and pageviews that the post has accumulated.
User reputation
The number next to a user's name is the sum of upvotes and accepted answers that user has collected.

Request new features
See the newsgroup or post in the Forum section of the site.
Keeping track of posts
You can bookmark any post, by clicking on the bookmark symbol . This will add the post to your bookmark tab that is accesible from your account. At the same time your activity tab on your profile page will show the responses that took place on the posts that you have interacted with. You can also subscribe to these messages as an RSS feed
Becoming a moderator
Active users above a certain reputation treshold are periodically promoted to moderators. You may also explicitly ask for moderation rights on the newsgroup.
Markup
Post may be formatted either with Markdown (default) or in Restructured Text Format
