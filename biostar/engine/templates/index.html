About this site

Data oriented sciences are experiencing a reproducibility crisis. It has become unexpectedly challenging to even understand how analyses are performed and even more difficult to repeat the same process again.

This site was designed to help scientists share, document, distribute and rerun data analysis scripts. We call these scripts recipes. Recipes are designed to facilitate the distribution, the sharing and the reuse of bioinformatics pipelines
